How to Use Our Website

  • Home Page: View a welcome message and learn about Cute Cubs Store.
  • Gallery Page: See images of our products and a table with product details like size and price.
  • Booking Page: Fill out the order form with your name, choose the product category, select for boy or girl, choose extras, and write any special comments. Click “Submit Order” when you are done.
  • Help Page: You are here! Read instructions on how to navigate and use each page easily.
  • About Page: Learn more about the creator of Cute Cubs Store and project details.

Form Instructions

  • All required fields are marked and must be filled before submitting.
  • Choose only one option for “For: Boy or Girl”.
  • You can select multiple extras like Gift Wrap or Express Delivery.
  • Write any additional requests in the Comments section.

If you need further help, please contact us through the form on the Booking page.
